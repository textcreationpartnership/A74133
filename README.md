#####Body#####

1. engraving of parliamentThe maner of siting of the Parlament of the Common-wealth of England. 1653.London printed & sould by P Stent.

    _ A List of the Parliament men.

    _ The Councel of State.

    _ A Committee for the affairs of Scotland.

    _ A Committee for the affaires of Ireland.

    _ A Committee for discovery of Bribery, fraud, breach of publick Trust, and publique Debts.

    _ A Committee for the Army.

    _ A Committee for the Law

    _ A Committee to consider of the Treasury.

    _ A Committee for advancement of Learning.

    _ A Committee for Tythes.

    _ A Committee for the poor, and regulating Commissions of Peace; for Alms-houses, and for Printing.

    _ A Committee for receiving Petitions.

    _ A Committee for Prisons and prisoners.

    _ A Committee for the business of Trade, Corporations, and for receiving Propositions for advance of the Commonwealth,
